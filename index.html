About Us

Int'ltec Group was established in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in 1995. the company is headquartered in Riyadh with offices all over the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia as well as in United Arab Emirates. Int'ltec Group is 100% Saudi-owned with a professional technical staff specialized in engineering design, project management, construction management, and electromechanical, and ICT services. The company's work force comprises of senior and medium level professionals as well as certified engineers and a proficient technical support team.
